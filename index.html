A Complex Farming Simulation

The perfect game to sit back, relax, and just farm.

Starting out, your grandfather has recently passed away and gave you his plot of land in Pelican Town. He didn't visit this land often and it is overgrown. Your job is to make friends with the townspeople, build up your own land with animals and crops, and rebuild the town's run-down community center.
